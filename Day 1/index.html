How to make a peanut butter and jelly sandwich

  1. Get out peanut butter, jelly, and 2 slices of bread.
  2. Spread peanut butter on once slice of bread and jelly on the other.
  3. Put both slices of bread together so the peanut butter and jelly are facing eachother.
  4. Enjoy!
